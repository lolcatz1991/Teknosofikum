News & Updates

Developing a concept for technology education for teachers in higher education, where broad interdisciplinarity is the special strength

August 2021

New paper out!

Magda Pischetola’s paper ‘Teacher professional development in higher education and the Teknosofikum project’ is now publicly available for reading and distribution. It gives an insight on the theoretical background of Teknosofikum and adress the data from the first prototype of Teknosofikum.

Keywords: Technology education, higher education, teacher professional development.

May 2021

First prototype

The first online prototype of Teknosofikum was tested with 7 participants from all participating institutions. With input from the participants we are developing the project further. In October and November we are taking in a new group of participants to test the updated format which includes new and updated content, and a mix of physical workshops and online self-study.

May 2020

Official Launch of the project

As a kick-off on the Teknosofikum project (2020-2023), we invited higher education educators and other higher education stakeholders to a webinar on digital bildung (“digital dannelse”) on the other side of Covid-19.

The webinar (video to the left) was the first in a series of Teknosofikum webinars that touch upon the content areas of the Teknosofikum project. The first webinar focused on Teknosofikum – what is it and how is it relevant?

  • Presentations (In Danish):
  • 0:00 Velkommen ved Mikala Hansbøl – Projektleder for Teknosofikum
  • 8:43 “Digital dannelse i de videregående uddannelser set fra Dansk ITs perspektiv” ved Rikke Hvilshøj – Direktør for Dansk IT
  • 22:12 ”Digital dannelse i de videregående uddannelser set fra DI’s perspektiv” ved Mette Fjord Sørensen – Uddannelseschef DI
  • 34:10 Paneldebat med Rikke Hvilshøj og Mette Fjord, moderator Lone Malmborg – Institutleder Digital Design
  • 53:50 ”Computational thinking og empowerment i de videregående uddannelser” ved Michael E. Caspersen – Æresprofessor ved Institut for Datalogi, Århus Universitet og Direktør for IT-Vest
  • 1:11:52 “Hvordan gør du programmering og it-tankegang relevant for studerende i mange forskellige videregående uddannelser?” ved Claus Brabrand – Lektor og leder af Center for Computing Education Research (CCER)
